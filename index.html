Speakers

David Pearson

As an esteemed book designer for Penguin and Editorial Zulma, Pearson strives to create designs that will intice bookstore windowshoppers to see more than just the covers he creates.

Archie Lee Coates IV

Cofounder of design studio PLAYLAB, INC. and CLOG, a semi-regular design publication, Archie Lee Coates IV is currently working on the NYC Public swimming project PLUS POOL.

Sheila Levrant de Bretteville

Levrant de Bretteville started her work in the design space in the early '60s, but her impact grew in 1971, when she "founded the first design program for women at CalArts" and shortly after, the Woman's Building, a "center for women's education and culture."
